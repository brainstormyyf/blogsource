## Configurations

You'll need to turn on the `markup.goldmark.parser.attribute.block`, to attach attributes on title and description.

> Similarly, you can attach the AOS animations on any content.

{{< bs/config-toggle hugo >}}
markup:
  goldmark:
    parser:
      attribute:
        block: true
        title: true
{{< /bs/config-toggle >}}
